Snow is type of weather that happens in the winter. Snow is ice crystals that come into our atmosphere. Snow melting temperature is 0 degrees celcius. Many places in the world snow all year long. Many sports come out of snow like snowboarding, skiing, and snowmachine. Snow is a great part of winter. Kids and sometimes adults will play in snow when there is enough of it. If you wear your coat, hat, gloves, snowshoes, snowpants, and a scarf, you should be fine to play in snow.
